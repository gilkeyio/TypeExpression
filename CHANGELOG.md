# Changelog

## 2.2.0 - 2025-06-07

### Added
- Left (`<<`) and right (`>>`) shift operators.

## 2.1.0 - 2025-06-06

### Added
- Introduced `^` as the bitwise XOR operator.

## 2.0.0 - 2025-06-05

### Breaking
- Exponentiation operator changed from `^` to `**`.
